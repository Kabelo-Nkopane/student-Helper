About St. Boniface High School

Our History

The Principal & Deputy Principal

Established in Maputsoe, Lesotho, St. Boniface High School has a long-standing reputation for academic excellence and holistic student development. The school operates under the motto "Thuto ke Leseli", which translates to "Education is Light". This reflects the school's commitment to illuminating the minds of its students with knowledge, values, and character.

Academic Excellence

St. Boniface High School offers a comprehensive curriculum that prepares students for university education and future careers. With an emphasis on both academics and extracurricular activities, the school nurtures talent and supports the growth of well-rounded individuals.

Our Facilities

The school is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, science labs, and sports fields. These resources provide an environment conducive to both learning and recreational development.

Community Engagement

As part of its commitment to societal growth, St. Boniface encourages students to participate in community service projects. These initiatives help cultivate empathy, responsibility, and active citizenship among students.
